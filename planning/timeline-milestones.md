# Timeline & Milestones  
**Sales Performance Optimization Initiative**

---

## Planning Approach
The project timeline was designed around phased delivery rather than fixed dates, allowing flexibility while maintaining structure. Milestones aligned with decision points and stakeholder feedback cycles.

---

## Project Phases & Milestones

### Phase 1: Discovery & Baseline (Weeks 1–2)
- Project charter approved
- Stakeholders identified and aligned
- Baseline KPI definitions established
- Initial risks documented

**Milestone:** Baseline performance framework confirmed

---

### Phase 2: Framework Design (Weeks 3–4)
- KPI framework finalized
- Reporting structure designed
- Interpretation guidelines documented
- Risk register updated

**Milestone:** Reporting framework ready for execution

---

### Phase 3: Execution & Monitoring (Weeks 5–6)
- Performance trends monitored
- Status reports delivered
- Early insights reviewed with stakeholders
- Adjustments made to analysis approach

**Milestone:** Actionable performance insights identified

---

### Phase 4: Optimization & Closeout (Weeks 7–10)
- Metrics refined based on usage
- Final documentation prepared
- Handoff completed
- Retrospective conducted

**Milestone:** Framework transitioned to operations

---

## Timeline Governance
- Progress reviewed through weekly status updates
- Milestones adjusted as needed based on data availability and feedback
- Focus maintained on outcomes rather than rigid scheduling

---

## Outcome
The phased timeline provided structure while allowing flexibility, supporting informed decision-making and successful project closeout.
