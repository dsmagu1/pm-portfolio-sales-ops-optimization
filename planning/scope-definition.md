# Scope Definition  
**Sales Performance Optimization Initiative**

---

## Purpose
This document defines the boundaries of the initiative to ensure alignment among stakeholders and prevent scope creep. Clear scope definition enabled focused delivery within existing operational constraints.

---

## In Scope
- Definition and documentation of sales performance KPIs
- Analysis of performance trends following operational changes
- Development of reporting structure and cadence
- Stakeholder communication and status reporting
- Risk identification and mitigation planning
- Documentation and handoff of deliverables

---

## Out of Scope
- Compensation or incentive program changes
- Policy, pricing, or underwriting decisions
- Engineering or system development
- Workforce staffing or scheduling redesign
- Individual performance management

---

## Assumptions
- Existing data sources remain available and reliable
- Operational changes continue during the project window
- Stakeholders engage in scheduled feedback cycles

---

## Constraints
- No authority to modify core systems or policies
- Project executed alongside ongoing operational responsibilities
- Limited ability to control external market or regulatory factors

---

## Scope Management Approach
- Scope reviewed during regular status updates
- Requests outside defined scope documented and deferred
- Focus maintained on visibility, analysis, and decision support

---

## Outcome
Clear scope definition enabled disciplined execution and reduced ambiguity, supporting timely delivery and stakeholder alignment.
